# 🎯 Next 10 Steps: The Cochran Firm: Legacy of Justice

This roadmap outlines the strategic progression for The Cochran Firm to uphold its legacy and expand its impact in the pursuit of justice.

## 🗺️ Execution Roadmap

1.  **⚖️ Define Primary Practice Areas**: Clearly delineate core specializations (e.g., Civil Rights, Personal Injury, Criminal Defense) to focus firm resources.
2.  **🛡️ Secure Malpractice Insurance**: Obtain comprehensive professional liability insurance that meets or exceeds bar association requirements.
3.  **📝 Establish Client Intake Protocols**: Develop a rigorous screening process and automated conflict-of-interest checks for all new inquiries.
4.  **💻 Implement Practice Management Software**: Deploy a secure, end-to-end legal software (e.g., Clio or MyCase) for document and case management. Use `LAW_FIRM_SOFTWARE_MVP.md` as the build-vs-buy and MVP delivery blueprint.
5.  **🌐 Launch Professional Digital Presence**: Develop a high-authority website and a strategic LinkedIn presence focusing on legal expertise and advocacy.
6.  **📑 Standardize Engagement Letters**: Create legally vetted, boilerplate fee agreements and engagement letters to streamline client onboarding.
7.  **🤝 Build Expert Witness Network**: Curate a database of vetted expert witnesses across medical, forensic, and technical fields.
8.  **🔐 Establish Secure Client Portal**: Implement an encrypted communication channel for clients to share documents and receive case updates.
9.  **🖊️ Launch Thought Leadership Strategy**: Start a legal blog or podcast to provide public education on civil rights and legal trends.
10. 💳 **Formalize Billing & Collections Workflow**: Implement a standardized monthly billing cycle with integrated electronic payment options for clients.
