Core Operating System

Client Intake and CRM

Mobile-first forms, autosaved drafts, contact history, family links, and fast search by name, phone, address, or case number.

Case Management

Status dashboards, next actions, assigned staff, deadlines, reminders, case timelines, and practice-area checklists.

Documents

Secure uploads, phone scan support, auto-naming, tags, templates, version history, and matter-level file export.

Billing and Payments

Flat fees, hourly time, invoice PDFs, payment plans, installment tracking, and manual recording for common payment methods.

Delivery

Build the parts that save time first.

The MVP is organized around staff workflows: capture the client, open the case, track the next action, store the document, bill the work, and preserve an audit trail.

  1. Sprint 0Foundation, roles, providers, statuses, schema, audit baseline.
  2. Sprint 1Mobile intake, autosave drafts, client profile, search, SMS consent.
  3. Sprint 2Case dashboard, deadlines, tasks, reminders, timeline, checklists.
  4. Sprint 3Secure uploads, tags, auto-naming, templates, version history.
  5. Sprint 4Fee settings, time entries, invoices, payment plans, payment records.
  6. Sprint 5RBAC, 2FA, audit review, backups, exports, limited pilot.
